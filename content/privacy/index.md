Your privacy matters. This site is intentionally simple, and I do **not** collect or share personal data about visitors.

## No analytics, no tracking

- This site does **not** use Google Analytics, Matomo, Hotjar, or any other tracking/analytics services.
- There are **no cookies**, no tracking pixels, and no third-party scripts designed to monitor your behavior.

## Minimal diagnostics for uptime & security

The only information that may be logged is the technical data needed to keep the site running securely and reliably, such as:

- Requests made to the server (URL, HTTP status, timestamp)
- User agent strings (to help block automated abuse)
- IP addresses (used temporarily by the hosting layer to mitigate bots and malicious traffic)
- Logs are regularly rotated

This data is used only to:

1. Ensure the site stays up and performs correctly
1. Protect the site from abuse and automated attacks

## No personal profiling or marketing

- No visitor profiles are built.
- No personal data is sold, shared, or used for marketing or advertising.
